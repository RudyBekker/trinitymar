    Lead Generation Methods

    By William Webb • 21 May, 2020 •
    I am sure if you were as sceptical as I were a few years back, you would have asked the above question more than once… Well, I hope I was not the only one, so allow me to shed some light for you on the different methodologies, both old school (which surprisingly a lot of companies are still using) to the more advances methodologies. There are basically 3 overarching lead generation methodologies, or simply put - methods to get more clients: 1. COLD CALLING FOR LEADS I really take my hat off to those poor people where companies are still stuck 10 years back where hardcore sales reps need to close the sale through cold calling. I do think this must surely be the worst job in the world, don’t you? It separates the wheat from the chaff. We're not fans of cold calling - we've tried it and it is soul-destroying. Plus, managing a host of sales reps who earn nothing but a commission isn't our idea of fun. We much prefer an automated approach to generating leads that is less reliant on the mood swings of a rep. And let's be honest: how many sales calls do you turn away every day? What is really absolutely astonishing, is the fact that some big corporates are still using this approach, just think what their poor employees must go through...says a lot how much they care about their people, doesn't it? 2. "SPRAY AND PRAY" The “spray and pray” approach might have worked for you in the past, but it’s not sustainable. To create a pipeline of qualified sales leads, you need to sweat it. You need to reach new prospects daily and, hopefully, turn them into paying, loyal, lifelong customers. This type of approach includes any traditional form of media like: Print Flyers Caxtons Newspapers magazines just to mention a few. The problem with this approach is that one simply cannot measure what return you are getting. Sure the publicists will tell you so many bought or watched, but they can never offer you a targeted qualifies audience and measurable returns like proper digital marketing lead generation offers, not to mention the fact that they are dying a slow death right now... 3. DIGITAL LEAD GENERATION Using Digital data points that all social media platforms provide combined with the perfect strategy and ideal copywriting, which entices your perfect client is the magic formula to this approach. So many try it, but so few get proper results. The beauty of lead generation lies in the fact that people are contacting you asking for quotes or more information. Because it's digital, with the right lead generation campaign, you can: Identify your goals, Determine your budget, Identify your target market's buying personas to create compelling messaging that appeals to each persona, Find the right channels that will reach them where they are - this might even be paper or street-based marketing overlaid with digital marketing, Set the duration of the campaign. Then voila, a constant stream of enquiries from people saying: 'Yes, I'm interested, please contact me!' We will naturally monitor the campaigns daily, making improvements and optimising them as we go. And then it’s up to you to follow up on all the sales leads that come pouring into your business. HERE'S WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW: The more you spend, the more leads you will get. We have some businesses who spend R5k per day on Google because that's where he gets all his business from. This is where one of our skills come into your benefit as we are experts in the field of maximising return on your ad spend through actively managing your ads ensuring they generate the required results you need. LEAD GENERATION AND YOUR CUSTOMER'S JOURNEY Creating customer enquiries means that your sales pipeline is constantly full of people who want to do business with you. But creating leads isn't the be-all and end-all. At the end of the day, all we're doing is sending customers your way, it's up to YOU and your business to follow up on those leads swiftly, and turn them into customers, and then delight them so that they turn into promoters of your business.
